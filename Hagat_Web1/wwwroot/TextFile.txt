With its know-how and experienced staff Hagat Defense Technologies can manufacture launchers, MRS pods and warheads of all sizes and diameters using filament winding method.
                                    <br />
                                    <br />
                                    So far, we have produced 107mm, 122mm, 302mm diameter launchers and pods for Multiple Launch Rocket Systems (MLRS). While manufacturing these launchers we used our own filament winding machine in the production process.
                                    <br />
                                    <br />
                                    We are also manufacturing warheads in various sizes with different production
                                    techniques. Till now we manufactured MK 82, MK 84 aircraft ammunitions.
                                    <br />
                                    <br />
                                    Apart from these, we have also manufacturing warheads in different weights and
                                    sizes designed to be launched from various Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs).
